Burrito

Description

  • whole meal is pushing 50+ grams of protien.
  • Can never say no to a burrito

Ingredients

  • pinto beans
  • flour tortillas
  • ground turkey
  • mozerella chz
  • Optional--red sauce *

ground turkey seasoning

  • onion powder
  • garlic powder
  • greek seasoning

Steps

  1. pour one tbsp of olive oil
  2. weigh out four ounces of ground turkey
  3. place turkey in pan and put stove on high
  4. throw on the all the seasoning and make sure to put plenty
  5. Put the stove on med-high and start working on the other tasks
  6. add pinto beans, garlic salt, and parmesan cheese to three flour tortillas
  7. Time it correctly so that when the ground turkey is cooked you can instantly add it to the hot tortillas

* < - You can't grab burrito with hands if you put red sauce all over it
